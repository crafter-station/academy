# Focused Visual Polish Plan

## Goal

Fix the three areas called out in the screenshots without reworking the full page again: the header, the Program cards, and the builder-fit section. Preserve the current Dub-derived typography/color foundation, Satoshi/Inter fonts, compact CTAs, content, links, and `/checkout` behavior.

## User Feedback Interpreted

- Header feels weird: current full Crafter Station logo plus divider plus detached `Academy` label reads like two disconnected brands and the nav is visually heavy.
- Program cards feel vibecoded: current orange/green/blue/purple top bars and filled numbered tiles feel like generic AI-generated SaaS cards.
- Builder-fit section can be better: current two equal checklist columns on a dot background feel basic and not premium enough.

## Files To Modify

- `src/app/page.tsx`
  - Header markup/classes around the sticky header.
  - `programCards` data shape if needed.
  - `ProgramCard` component and Program section wrapper.
  - `Checklist` replacement or new fit-panel component.
  - Builder-fit section markup.
- `src/app/globals.css`
  - Optional only: add a subtle warm neutral accent token/utility if cleaner than inline values.

Do not modify `src/app/checkout/route.ts`.

## Header Redesign

Replace the current full-wordmark + divider + `Academy` split with a calmer, intentional product lockup.

Recommended implementation:

- Use the Crafter Station icon or smaller logo treatment instead of full wordmark plus divider.
- Build a single left lockup:
  - Icon/mark at `h-5` or `h-6`.
  - Text: `Crafter Academy` or `Academy` in one contiguous label.
  - Optional tiny parent label: `by Crafter Station` as muted microcopy only if it fits.
- Remove the vertical divider.
- Reduce nav weight:
  - `text-sm`, `font-medium`, `text-[rgb(var(--content-default))]`.
  - Gap around `gap-5`, not `gap-7`.
- Use a subtle nav capsule on desktop:
  - `rounded-full border border-[rgb(var(--border-subtle))] bg-white px-1.5 py-1`.
  - Links get `rounded-full px-3 py-1.5` and hover to `bg-[rgb(var(--bg-subtle))]`.
- Keep the CTA compact, but change label from `Reserve` to `Reserve seat` for clarity.
- Keep mobile simple: logo lockup left, `Reserve seat` button right, hide nav.

Why: This removes the awkward duplicate brand lockup and makes the header feel like a designed product rail instead of a generic heavy navbar.

## Program Cards Redesign

Replace the four isolated rainbow feature cards with one cohesive curriculum sequence panel.

Recommended implementation:

- Change `programCards` from loud accent colors to restrained neutral/warm metadata.
- Remove these current patterns:
  - Saturated top bar.
  - Filled colorful numbered square.
  - Hover lift with big shadow.
- Create one parent shell:
  - `overflow-hidden rounded-[1.75rem] border border-[rgb(var(--border-subtle))] bg-white shadow-[0_1px_2px_rgba(0,0,0,0.03)]`.
  - Inside: `grid md:grid-cols-2 lg:grid-cols-4`.
  - Add internal borders/dividers between steps.
- Each step becomes an editorial curriculum cell:
  - Small neutral index pill: `01`, `02`, `03`, `04`.
  - Optional tiny muted warm accent rail or dot using a single color such as `#b97044` at low prominence.
  - Title in Satoshi medium, body in Inter muted gray.
  - Optional footer label like `Product`, `System`, `Demo`, `Launch` to reinforce sequence.
- Color direction:
  - Use neutral surfaces and borders.
  - Use one muted copper/warm accent only as a tiny dot/rail, not a fill.
  - Avoid rainbow colors and saturated gradient/shadow effects.
- Responsive:
  - Mobile stacks each cell with separators.
  - Tablet 2x2.
  - Desktop 4-column connected panel.

Why: It turns the cards from decorative SaaS feature cards into a coherent curriculum/product-loop artifact.

## Builder-Fit Section Redesign

Replace the two equal checklist columns with a single premium “fit diagnostic” panel.

Recommended implementation:

- Keep the left editorial copy, but remove the current dot-matrix dominance if it feels noisy.
- Right side becomes one unified white assessment module:
  - Shell: `rounded-[1.75rem] border border-[rgb(var(--border-default))] bg-white shadow-[0_18px_60px_rgba(0,0,0,0.06)]`.
  - Header row: `Builder fit signal` plus muted badge `Selective cohort`.
  - Subcopy: `What the cohort is calibrated for.`
- Inside panel:
  - Primary group: `Strong fit` with the three `builderFit` items.
  - Secondary group: `Not calibrated for` with the three `notFor` items.
  - Use rows with subtle separators, not individual cards.
  - Positive marker: small charcoal `+` or check in a neutral outlined circle.
  - Negative marker: muted dash/minus in gray.
  - Avoid green/gray dot checklist styling.
- Optional premium composition:
  - Strong-fit rows get white background.
  - Not-fit rows sit in a `bg-[rgb(var(--bg-subtle))]` inset block.
- Responsive:
  - Desktop: left copy and right diagnostic panel in asymmetric grid.
  - Mobile: copy first, diagnostic panel below.

Why: Same content, but reframed as a product-style selection signal instead of basic checklist cards.

## Design Constraints

- Preserve Dub-derived typography:
  - Inter body/UI.
  - Satoshi headings.
  - Normal tracking.
  - Compact buttons.
- Keep color discipline:
  - Neutrals first: `#fafafa`, `#fff`, `#f5f5f5`, `#e5e5e5`, `#d4d4d4`, `#171717`, `#525252`, `#737373`.
  - One muted warm accent is acceptable for tiny details.
  - No rainbow cards, saturated tiles, or decorative gradient fills in these sections.
- Preserve links:
  - `/checkout`.
  - Scholarship mailto.
  - Notion syllabus link.
